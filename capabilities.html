Capabilities / Analytical Approach

Service Descriptor

Independent analytical consulting through advanced biochemical, neurofunctional, and genomic case analysis.

CIBNA is a boutique analytical center built around a personalized, high-depth approach to case evaluation, centered on a proprietary analytical framework — a high-resolution methodology designed to interpret clinical complexity beyond the scope of conventional, functional, or integrative models.

The CIBNA analytical framework was created by Elena Maleva, MD (Non-US), PhD, Medical Analyst with advanced training in Functional Nutrition and Genomics, and reflects years of work with multi-layered clinical data, scientific research, surgical expertise, and extensive experience in high-complexity case interpretation, where patient history, laboratory findings, biochemical signatures, genomic insights, and physiological patterns are woven into a coherent analytical narrative.

Our work is sought out by individuals and clinicians facing complex, non-standard clinical situations — cases in which existing data is inconclusive, interpretations across disciplines diverge, and clear clinical direction is difficult to establish.

CIBNA supports physicians by undertaking the in-depth analytical work required by complex cases, synthesizing multidimensional biochemical, neurofunctional, and genomic data alongside focused review of relevant scientific literature, to clarify unresolved patterns and contribute to further clinical interpretation.
